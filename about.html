About Plato Trillion

Building the visibility layer for the physical world.

The systems that move the economy — warehouses, fleets, venues, factories — still run with surprising blind spots. We're changing that.

Our mission

Plato Trillion was founded on a simple observation: the most important things happening inside an operation are often the hardest to see. A misrouted pallet, a bottleneck at a dock, a safety risk on a busy floor — by the time these surface in a report, the moment to act has passed.

We build intelligent monitoring systems that close that gap. By combining purpose-built hardware with software that turns continuous coverage into clear, timely signals, we give operations teams something they've never really had: a live, trustworthy picture of what's happening across every site they run.

Today, teams across logistics, events, and industry rely on Plato Trillion to keep their environments visible, coordinated, and accountable — wherever the work happens.
